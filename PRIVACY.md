# Privacy Policy

AutoGrouping processes tab URLs locally only to match user-defined grouping rules.

- No data is sold.
- No data is transmitted to an external server.
- No analytics, advertising, or telemetry is included.
- No page content, cookies, browsing history, or screenshots are collected.
- Rules are stored in Chrome Sync storage when available.
- Session-only ownership and protection state is stored in Chrome Session storage.
